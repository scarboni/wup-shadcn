**Date:** March 8, 2026
**Sites analyzed:** Alibaba.com, Faire.com, Merkandi.com (limited — blocked by Cloudflare)
**Compared against:** WholesaleUp `/deal/[slug]` page

---

## Summary

Our deal page already covers the core wholesale product listing fields well — pricing, MOQ, volume tiers, supplier info, shipping, payment, platform profit comparison, and product identifiers. However, competitors surface several additional data points that we're missing. The gaps fall into five categories: **trust & social proof**, **product specifications**, **logistics detail**, **buyer engagement features**, and **supplier depth**.

## Recommended Priority Actions

### High Priority (significant competitive gaps)

1. **Structured product specifications** — Replace or supplement freeform description with a structured key-value attributes table. This enables filtering and comparison.

2. **Product variants/options** — Add support for selectable variants (size, color, fragrance, specification) that affect pricing and availability.

3. **Buyer reviews on products** — Even simple star ratings + text reviews on individual deals would match both Alibaba and Faire.

4. **Supplier company metrics** — Surface operational stats (response time, on-time delivery rate, years active, staff count, revenue tier) in the supplier sidebar.

### Medium Priority (nice-to-have competitive features)

5. **Return/refund policy display** — Show whether returns are accepted, under what conditions, and any buyer protection guarantees.

6. **Video support in gallery** — Allow video alongside product images.

7. **Stock urgency indicators** — "Only X left" labels and "Bestseller" badges to drive conversion.

8. **Packaging dimensions & weight** — Help buyers estimate shipping costs.

9. **Certification badges** — Visual badges (CE, FCC, ISO, etc.) instead of text-only mentions.

10. **Estimated delivery date** — Compute and show an actual delivery date range, not just "ships in X days".

11. **Wishlist / Save for later** — Let buyers bookmark deals for comparison.

12. **Customization/white-label options** — Show available branding and packaging add-ons with pricing.

### Low Priority (differentiators but not critical)

13. **"New" and "Bestseller" product badges**
14. **Frequently bought together / cross-sell**
15. **Product language indicator**
16. **Ingredients/materials structured field**
17. **Buy-now-pay-later / financing options**
18. **Share button**
19. **Tester/sample ordering option**
20. **Promotional pricing events / seasonal discounts**

## What We Have That Competitors Don't

These are genuine competitive advantages worth highlighting in marketing:

- **Platform profit calculator** (Amazon, eBay markup/profit per unit) — neither Alibaba nor Faire offer this
- **TARIC customs codes** — critical for cross-border EU trade
- **EAN/SKU identifiers** — important for retail listing
- **Supplier opening hours with timezone** — unique real-time availability indicator
- **Document attachments** (PDFs, spreadsheets) — product documentation downloads
- **Dropship availability flag** — neither competitor surfaces this clearly
- **Negotiable price flag** — signals deal flexibility
- **Product grade/condition** (shared with Merkandi, absent from Alibaba/Faire)
